HOW COURSE SCOPING WORKS (for future edits)
-------------------------------------------
`courses[]` (in lib/topics.ts) lists each course's section ids in order.
page.tsx derives `courseSections`/`courseTopicIds` from the selected course
and feeds them to the sidebar, search, dashboard completion, and radar.
Adding a section to a course = add its id to that course's `sectionIds`.
